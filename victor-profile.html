About Our Groomer

Victor Soto

My name is Victor Manuel Soto, I’ve been a pet groomer for the last 10 years. I was born In Brooklyn, New York but currently reside in Queens, New York. I have three dogs; two girls (Luna and Annie) and one boy (Max) and love baseball… let’s go Mets!!! My passion for my career motivates me every day and I love what I do. I love spreading my passion for animals and influencing the youth on achieving their life aspirations.
