About Us

In August 2020, we started Fetch with the goal of revolutionizing the way dogs are groomed. From then on, we have stopped at nothing to create the most unique and loving experience for your beloved four-legged family members. We have educated ourselves and searched the world for the highest quality ethically-sourced raw materials to best serve dogs. We use our rich products and techniques to transform the way your dogs look and feel. We want your fur children to leave Fetch, embracing their clean coats and feeling like brand new, healthy and happy pups. The Fetch experience is more than just grooming. It is a luxurious ritual of peace.
