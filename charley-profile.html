About Our Groomer

Charley Ukwubiwe

Hi dog lovers! I’m definitely one of you! I like to say I’ve been in the dog care business since birth! I grew up with my very own puppy brother, Oliver, till we were both 12 years old. When he passed, I occupied my time with neighborhood dog walking and sitting for six years. I went off to university to pursue a career as a vet. It was then when I worked as a part-time dog and cat groomer. I learned from the best and was able to conceptualize my own luxury experience for our furry friends. I incorporate medicated baths, massage techniques taken from around the world, and also psycho therapy. Canine mental health matters! After undergrad, I pivoted to a more business-oriented mindset with all that I learned as a groomer. I obtained an MBA from Columbia and co-founded Fetch with Pho, Alexandria and Victor. I look forward to caring for your dog pals in a way that will blow your minds.
